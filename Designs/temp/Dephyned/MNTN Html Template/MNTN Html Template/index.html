What we do

Sketch & Design

After we have the general idea of what you want, we sketch out and design the product from start to finish.

Prototyping

Prototyping is the getting the overall layout and functionality of the application drawn out. This is the first step.

Development

This is the final step before you get your finished product. This step involves actually coding and implement the graphics and UI for your DEPHYNED application.

iPhone

iOS leads the mobile world in sleekness, and design. iPhone applications provide the user with a small, easy to handle device that allows great functionality.

iPad

Often times you want your iOS app to offer more functionality that looks nicer on a larger screen. That's when you want us for your iPad development.

Web

Web sites are essential to your business advertisement, and web apps are becoming more and more popular largely because of their cross-platform ability. Let us know if this is what you desure!

About Us

We are a group of elite developers and designers who specialize in developing applications that make their customers say WOW!

We are based out of Las Vegas, a leading location of desiogn innovation. We look at everything from nice cars, to beautiful animals in order to receive inspiration for our designs. DEPHYNED knows that the most important aspect to an app or a website is the look and feel.

Beautiful design is a necessity, not a luxury. If it's not beautiful, then it shouldn't exist. That's the way we feel at DEPHYNED.
